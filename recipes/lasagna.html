Lasagna

Description

This is how to make lasagna. Here's a very brief overview of what you can

expect when you make homemade lasagna:

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook noodles
  3. Layer the noodles and meat
  4. Bake the lasagna
  5. Enjoy!
